HOW WILL CLIMATE CHANGE AFFECT ME?

Why increasing global temperatures are problematic:
a. rising sea levels could lead to flooding
b. some areas more prone to drought, loss of crops, starvation
c. destruction of ecosystems, cascading effect leading to mass extinction, could affect humans too.

1. Health Impacts: Climate change worsens air pollution and heat-related illnesses, and increases the spread of diseases carried by insects, affecting the public health.

2.Economic Effects: It can lead to job losses, damages to property and infrastructure from extreme weather, and increases in food prices due to affected agriculture.

3.Environmental Changes: Climate change results in more frequent natural disasters, biodiversity loss, and altered ecosystems, impacting outdoor activities and natural resources.

4. Social and Psychological Effects: The stress and anxiety from experiencing or anticipating climate change impacts, alongside the strain on community cohesion from migration and displacement, affect mental well-being and social structures.

(WWF)
